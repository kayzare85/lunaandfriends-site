Wrist Racer

Privacy Policy

Last updated 8 July 2026

Wrist Racer is developed by Luna & Friends Ltd. We built it to be played, not to collect your data. This policy explains what little there is to explain.

What we collect

Nothing, directly. Wrist Racer has no accounts, no sign-ups, and no servers of our own. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal information.

What stays on your device

Your game progress — coins, unlocked cars, settings, and stats — is stored locally on your Apple Watch and iPhone, and synced between them directly using Apple's WatchConnectivity. It never reaches us.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see your payment details. Purchase records are managed by Apple under the Apple Media Services Terms.

Game Center

If you use leaderboards, they are provided by Apple's Game Center. Your Game Center profile and scores are handled by Apple under Apple's privacy policy. We do not receive your personal Game Center data.

Analytics and advertising

Wrist Racer contains no third-party analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind. We may see anonymous, aggregated crash reports and statistics that Apple provides to all developers from users who have opted in to sharing diagnostics with Apple — these contain no personal information.

Children

Wrist Racer is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, children included.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
