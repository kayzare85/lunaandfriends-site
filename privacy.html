Privacy

Last updated 6 July 2026

This is the plain-English version, because that's the only version we have. Luna & Friends Ltd makes cartoons, toys, and games. Right now our first app is Wrist Racer, and here is exactly what happens to your information when you use it.

What Wrist Racer stores

Wrist Racer keeps your game data — scores, coins, unlocks, and settings — on your device only. It stays on your Apple Watch and iPhone. We never see it, and it is never sent to us.

What we don't do

  • No accounts. There's nothing to sign up for and no login.
  • No tracking. We don't follow what you do in the app or anywhere else.
  • No analytics. We don't run any usage or measurement tools.
  • No personal data collected by us. We don't gather your name, email, contacts, location, or anything else.

Purchases and leaderboards

Any purchases in Wrist Racer, and any future leaderboard or Game Center features, are handled by Apple. When Apple processes those, your information is covered by Apple's privacy policy, not by us. We don't receive your payment details.

Children

Because we collect no personal data at all, Wrist Racer collects nothing from children either. Purchases remain under the control of Apple and your device's parental settings.

Changes

If we ever change how any of this works, we'll update this page and the date at the top.
